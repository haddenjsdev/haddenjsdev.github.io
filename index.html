About

How I became a Fullstack Developer.

  • 2013-2014

    Founded company, built 200+ client base

    I founded a company that served the music industry. We used data analysis & KPI's to help musicians grow their brand and simplify their business funnctions. This was my introduction to SEO, UI & UX design.

  • Jan-June 2015

    Leveraging SEO, UI/UX & marketing became my main job.

    I went from 12 hour days recording clients in music studios, to 15 hour days building simple websites, developing SEO strategies in 2015, getting music to people required a pallet of unique tech and marketing skills. More and more I found mysel. enjoying that part of the process more then the music creation.

  • July 2015 - March 2016

    Began side projects, entered dev community.

    w 3

    I transistioned from designing only when I had to doing it for fun on the side. At this point I was only using Photoshop, basic HTML and CSS. The more I learned about the community the more I wanted to learn how computers, the internet and programs they ran on worked.

  • April-present 2017

    became a full-stack developer

    I took MIT courses on o-notation(algorithms), Harvard courses on syntax, and began building fullstack applications Ruby and PHP before settling into Javacript, Node.js, Angular.js and React.js
